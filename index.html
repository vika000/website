About me


My name is Vika. I am a visual Artist, so I spend most of my time painting and drawing in my studio. I was born on February 12th, at 9:30pm in a small room at Sunny side hospital. Consequently, I am an Aquarius sun, Libra rising and Scorpio moon. Astrology is a very fascinating lens to view the world through and I’ve found myself able to connect with strangers through conversations related to the zodiac.
I studied Video and sound at PNCA and I have been taking classes here at PCC. I found myself drawn to the world of computers through a class entitled “coding for artists.” Ive been coding for a couple of years now using the programs Max MSP, Processing and P5.js. I took computer information systems because I wanted to learn more about coding.
